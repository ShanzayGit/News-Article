The FIFA World Cup 2026 opening ceremony marks the beginning of a historic tournament hosted across three countries, celebrating football, culture and global unity. Spectacular performances, vibrant visuals and the energy of fans create an unforgettable atmosphere before the first kick-off symbolizing how the sport connects millions of people around the world.

With world-renowned artists, immersive stage productions and celebrations across North America, the ceremony showcases the excitement and diversity that make the World Cup special. It sets the tone for weeks of competition, passion and memorable moments on football’s biggest stage.
